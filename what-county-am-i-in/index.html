What County Am I In? Find Out Instantly

Whether you're driving through unfamiliar territory, hunting or fishing in a new area, or need to know the county for a legal or administrative reason — County Names & Lines shows you exactly which county you're in the moment you open the app.

Instant county detection from GPS

Open the app and your current county appears immediately, detected from your iPhone's GPS location. No typing, no searching — just open the app and see your county name, state, and its position on the map.

The county name is displayed prominently at the top of the screen alongside the county seat and the county's approximate area. The map shows your precise location relative to the county boundaries.

Seeing county lines on the map

The interactive map overlays all US county boundaries, with your current county highlighted. Zoom in to see where exactly you are relative to the county line — useful when you're near a border and need to know which side you're on.

As you move (driving, hiking, or flying), the county display updates automatically. Crossing a county line shows the new county name immediately with the transition clearly visible on the map.

Why you might need to know your county

County matters more than people realize: voting registrations, property taxes, hunting and fishing licenses, court jurisdictions, emergency services, school districts, and permit applications all operate at the county level. Knowing your county quickly and accurately avoids mistakes in all of these contexts.

For travelers, county information can also determine which local regulations apply — speed limits on county roads, permitted camping areas, and wildlife management zones.

Works offline in remote areas

County boundaries are stored locally on your device, so the app works without a cell signal or internet connection. This is particularly valuable for hunters, hikers, and outdoor enthusiasts who need county information in areas with poor or no coverage.

The map tiles for your current region download when you have a connection and remain available offline. Zoom level and coverage area are configurable for pre-download before heading into the backcountry.
